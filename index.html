Our Portfolio

Innovative AI-Driven Devices for Advanced Healthcare Solutions

At Warnal Labs Pvt Ltd, we specialize in developing state-of-the-art AI-based hardware devices that enhance medical diagnostics and patient care. Our portfolio showcases our commitment to innovation and excellence in the healthcare industry. Here are some of our flagship products:

1. Pupilometer
Description:
Our Pupilometer is a cutting-edge device designed to measure pupillary reflexes, providing critical insights into neurological conditions. This non-invasive tool is essential for healthcare professionals in assessing brain function and detecting potential issues early.
Key Features:
Accurate Measurements: Delivers precise readings of pupillary response times and sizes.
AI-Powered Analysis: Utilizes advanced AI algorithms to interpret data and provide detailed reports.
Portable and User-Friendly: Compact design for easy use in various medical settings, from emergency rooms to outpatient clinics.

2. Anemometer
Description:
The Anemometer is an innovative device that measures hemoglobin levels from eye conjunctival images. Utilizing AI technology, it offers a non-invasive and efficient method for diagnosing and monitoring anemia.
Key Features:
Non-Invasive Testing: Eliminates the need for blood draws, making it safer and more comfortable for patients.
AI Integration: Advanced image processing and machine learning algorithms provide accurate hemoglobin level estimations.
Quick and Reliable: Delivers fast results, allowing for timely diagnosis and treatment decisions.

3. Colposcope
Description:
Our Colposcope is designed to take high-resolution images of the cervix, providing crucial data for the early detection and diagnosis of cervical conditions. With AI-based inferences, it enhances the accuracy and reliability of medical assessments.
Key Features:
High-Resolution Imaging: Captures detailed images of the cervix for thorough examination.
AI-Based Inferences: Employs sophisticated AI to analyze images and provide diagnostic insights.
User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design ensures ease of use for healthcare providers, improving workflow efficiency.
